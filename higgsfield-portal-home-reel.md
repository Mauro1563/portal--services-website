# Higgsfield Storyboard — Portal Home 30s Promo Reel

A production-ready storyboard for generating a 30-second promotional video for **Portal Home** (portalservices.digital), the all-in-one SaaS for cleaning businesses serving owners, cleaners, and customers.

---

## How to use

1. Open a new chat at **claude.ai** (use the web app — **not** Claude Code, since Higgsfield MCP runs there). Confirm the Higgsfield MCP server is connected (check the tools panel for `higgsfield_*` actions).
2. Paste each **Higgsfield prompt** below one scene at a time (or batch them in a single message if your model supports parallel tool calls). Let Higgsfield render each 3–6s clip and download the MP4s as they finish.
3. Drop all six clips into any NLE (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, Premiere, Final Cut). Concatenate in scene order, add the suggested transitions + music + voiceover, and export per the editing notes below.

## Music suggestion

- **Genre**: Upbeat, modern corporate-indie / "tech-optimism" — think Stripe, Linear, or Notion product films.
- **Tempo**: 110–120 BPM, with a soft build into a clean drop around the 14s mark (Scene 4) and a satisfying resolve at 26s (logo outro).
- **Mood**: Warm synth pads, plucked guitar or marimba arpeggios, a four-on-the-floor kick that kicks in mid-reel, gentle airy claps.
- **Free-license sources**:
  - **Uppbeat.io** — search "corporate uplifting", "tech startup", "modern brand" (free with attribution on the free plan).
  - **Pixabay Music** — search "corporate motivational" (CC0, no attribution needed).
  - **YouTube Audio Library** — filter "Inspirational / Bright" + 110–120 BPM.
  - **Artlist.io** or **Epidemic Sound** if you have a paid sub — search "uplifting startup tech".

Recommended track shape: 4-bar intro (Scene 1) → main groove enters at Scene 2 → energy lift at Scene 4 → tail-out chord at logo outro.

## Editing notes

**Concatenation order**: Scene 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 (strict chronological — no re-ordering).

**Transitions**:
- Scene 1 → 2: **Cross-fade (12 frames / 0.5s)** — let particles dissolve into the phone shot.
- Scene 2 → 3: **Hard cut on the beat** (sync to music kick at 8s).
- Scene 3 → 4: **Whip-pan / motion blur transition (6 frames)** to suggest movement from indoors to outdoors.
- Scene 4 → 5: **Hard cut on the beat** (around 20s).
- Scene 5 → 6: **White-flash cross-fade (8 frames)** — the confetti burst justifies a soft bloom into the warm white outro.

**Title card placement**:
- No title cards needed during 0–26s (let the UI mockups do the talking).
- Logo + tagline + URL only on the final 26–30s outro card.
- Optional lower-third caption at Scene 2 (4s): "portalservices.digital" in a thin gradient bar — small, bottom-center, fades after 2s.

**Subtitles / captions**:
- Burn in subtitles for the voiceover (most social viewers watch muted). Use Inter Medium, white with a soft drop shadow, centered, bottom-third safe area.
- Export a Spanish subtitle track AND an English subtitle track as separate `.srt` sidecars for accessibility.

**Export format**:
- **Master**: 1080×1920 (9:16), H.264, 30fps, 12–16 Mbps, AAC 256kbps stereo, MP4 container — for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts.
- **Web hero variant**: 1920×1080 (16:9), H.264, 30fps, 8–10 Mbps, MP4 — for the portalservices.digital homepage hero (muted autoplay, looping the first 8s).
- **Square variant** (optional): 1080×1080 (1:1) for LinkedIn / Twitter feed.
- **Color space**: Rec. 709, 8-bit. Limited range (16–235) for broadcast safety.
- **Audio**: -14 LUFS integrated (social standard), true-peak -1 dBTP, no clipping.

**Final QA checklist**:
- [ ] Logo readable in first frame and last frame
- [ ] URL `portalservices.digital` visible for at least 3 seconds total across the reel
- [ ] All UI mockups pixel-sharp (no compression mush on text)
- [ ] Voiceover synced to within 100ms of visual cue
- [ ] Music ducked -6dB under voiceover
- [ ] Captions burn-in matches spoken track word-for-word
- [ ] Export validated on phone (not just desktop) before publishing
